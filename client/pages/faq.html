Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Braintube.

General Questions
What is Braintube?
Braintube is an educational platform designed specifically for VTU students. We provide high-quality study materials including notes, previous year question papers, syllabus information, and curated "Fix Questions" to help you excel in your exams.
Is Braintube affiliated with VTU?
No, Braintube is not officially affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University. We are an independent educational platform created by students and educators to support VTU students in their academic journey.
Which branches and semesters do you cover?
We cover all major branches including CSE, ISE, AI&ML, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, and Data Science. Our materials span from Semester 1 to Semester 8 following the VTU 2022 scheme.
Fix Questions
What are "Fix Questions"?
Fix Questions are carefully curated important questions for each subject, selected based on analysis of previous VTU exam patterns, syllabus weightage, and topic importance. They help you focus your preparation on high-probability questions.
Will these exact questions appear in my exam?
We cannot guarantee that exact questions will appear in your exam. However, our Fix Questions are based on thorough analysis and cover important concepts that are frequently tested. Many students have found them helpful in their preparation.
How do I receive Fix Questions after purchase?
After successful payment, the Fix Questions PDF will be sent to your registered email address within 24 hours (usually much faster). Check your spam/promotions folder if you don't see it in your inbox.
