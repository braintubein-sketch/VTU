Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address when you make a purchase or contact us
  • Payment information processed securely through Razorpay
  • Subject preferences and branch/semester information
  • Messages and feedback you send to us

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver purchased study materials via email
  • To send order confirmations and receipts
  • To respond to your questions and support requests
  • To improve our services and content offerings
  • To send occasional updates about new materials (you can opt out)

3. Payment Security

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store your credit/debit card details on our servers. Razorpay handles all sensitive payment information with bank-level security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay) to complete transactions
  • Email services to deliver your purchased materials
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
